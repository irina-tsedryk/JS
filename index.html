About Us

Dr. Richard Fernandes, PhD, MBA - Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder

With a first class honours BSc degree (Science), a.Ph.D (Biochemistry) and an honours MBA. Luxcel co-founder and CEO, Dr Richard S. Fernandes, has spent his entire career in the Life Sciences.

Dr Fernandes has extensive experience within FDA approved pharmaceutical facilities (Schering Plough, Cork; Leo Laboratories, Dublin, Ireland). in areas such as Analytical Method Development, Validation. Technology Transfer and in implementing working practices and systems.

Prior to his work in industry, Dr Fernandes held post-doctoral research and lecturing positions in Uniyersity College Cork. As a post-graduate student in the lab of Prof Tom Cotter, Dr Femandes specialized in the field of Apoptosis and published in a number of peer-reyiewed Journals.

Professor Dmitri В. Papkovsky - Chief Scientific Officer and Co-Founder

Prof papkovsky has long been involved in the research on the phosphorescent porphyrin probes. optical oxygen sensing, fluorescence spectroscopy and their bioanalytical, diagnostic and screening applications.

He has 80+ journal papers and books, 8 patents. He is also in charge of the Biophysics and Bioanalysis research lab at Biochemistry Department, University College Cork.

Prof papkoysky IS the inventor of Luxcel RST and Label Technology.
